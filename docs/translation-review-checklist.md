# Translation review checklist

Review the MVP dictionaries before launch:

- Confirm product category names use consistent terms in `en`, `ru`, and `am`.
- Confirm order and payment status labels match admin operations.
- Confirm generation terms use credits consistently and distinguish prompts, previews, and production review.
- Confirm Armenian uses app locale `am` while formatting uses `hy-AM`.
- Confirm missing keys fall back to English rather than breaking the UI.
- Confirm public storefront pages do not show mojibake or replacement characters.
