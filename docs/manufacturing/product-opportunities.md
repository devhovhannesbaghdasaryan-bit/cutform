# Manufacturable Product Opportunities

Date: 2026-06-19

This list uses the shop capabilities in `docs/manufacturing/tools.json` and current web research signals. The strongest demand themes found were personalization/custom gifts, warm wood and vintage-inspired decor, Neo Deco/geometric forms, wide-format signage, short-run customization, and practical 3D-printed parts.

## Review Notes

- Child-facing, medical/adaptive, load-bearing, food-contact, electrical, and safety-critical products need admin/manual review before selling.
- Several machines still need exact manuals and shop-tested presets before publishing guaranteed material/thickness limits.
- The fastest path is to build repeatable templates first, then offer personalization fields for names, logos, dimensions, colors, and finishes.
